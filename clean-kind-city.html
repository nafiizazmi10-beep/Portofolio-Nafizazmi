Kind Hunters

Tentang Proyek

Kind Hunter is a game created using Roblox Studio. This game is designed with a meaningful purpose: to educate and inspire children to develop positive habits in their daily lives. The main focus of the game is to encourage players to throw trash in the proper place and to help other people whenever they can.

Fitur utama:

  • Laporan warga real-time
  • Bank sampah digital dengan poin reward
  • Peta interaktif fasilitas umum
  • Forum diskusi lingkungan

Video Proyek

Kind Hunters Through interactive gameplay, Kind Hunter teaches environmental awareness in a fun and engaging way. Players complete missions that involve cleaning up litter, keeping areas tidy, and protecting the environment from pollution. By turning these actions into game objectives, children can learn the importance of responsibility and cleanliness while still enjoying themselves.
